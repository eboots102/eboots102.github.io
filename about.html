WHAT IS E-BOOTS?

E-boots are the shoes that designed and developed excellently. This shoe emphasizes the center of gravity of the foot and mechanism to maintain the balance of body to prevent the accident from falling.

If you stumble or walk, you won’t be able to fall down. It also has good footwear. Slip resistant as well.

E-boots is suitable for all ages and very useful for those who can’t walk so well, such as child, older people, people who are in physical therapy and have weak muscles to travel and move more safely.

MATERIALS

Shoes and laces are made from Dyneema fibers, One of the world's strongest materials.

Dyneema is 15x stronger than steel. So, E-BOOTS is durable but flexible , tighten the foot and has good ventilation. A sole made from special rubber with a modern synthesis, that makes the new polymer arrangement. It's very lightweight and has good support. A button made of plastic that Insulated.

FUNCTION OF E-BOOTS

1. When accident occurs, a counterfort will support the force to protect ankle. And a couterfort is designed for help people who have a problem with movement to balance their body.

 

2. A sole and insole are made from special rubber that can protect slip and corrosion of tread.

 

3. E-BOOTS has automatic rope tying system to tie and unleash laces when you press a button.

 

4. This shoes has Fall Protection System. System is automatic active since you wear the shoes but you can turn it off.

 

5. Have  amoled display for beauty.

HOW TO OPERATE

- First, wear the shoes.

- Second, press the button at side of shoes to tie laces. Make sure the shoes fit with your feet.

- Then, turn on or turn off the Fall Protection System. Up to wearer.

- Finally, check the signal status is it ready. If it has green light means ready.

- Works normally.

WARNING!

1. Beware your hands while automatic rope tying system is working. It may cause injury.

2. Don't modify the shoes. All system might work unproperly.

3. If automatic system have objections , should not wear.
